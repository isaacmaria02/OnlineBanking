FAQ's

How to Open a new Savings Acount?

Follow the steps given below to open a new Savings Account

  1. Visit SBBI website
  2. Click on the Login/Register button on the top-right corner
  3. Fill in the required details such as name,email and aadhar card number
  4. After clicking on submit,you will receive your Account number on your registered e-mail address
  5. Congratultions,your account is now active :)

How to Register for Internet Banking?

  1. Once your Account is Activated,you can opt for Internet Banking Service
  2. Set your User ID & transaction password
  3. Note your User ID/Transaction as it will be used for all further transcations.
  4. Fill in the required details and click 'Submit'
  5. Your Internet Banking facility is activated

How to Login?

  1. Visit SBBI website
  2. Click on the Login/Register button on the top-right corner
  3. Enter your UserID and password
  4. If your ID and password are correct you will be redirected to your Dashboard
